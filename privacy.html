Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using the app, you may provide certain Personal Information, including but not limited to the API Keys you provide, cryptocurrencies digital wallet addresses (from now on “Wallet Addresses”) and cryptocurrencies holdings (from now on “Holdings”).

THE PERSONAL INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE THROUGH THE APP IS NOT COLLECTED BY OR TRANSMITTED TO BITSNAPP IN ANY WAY. THE ONLY INFORMATION WE RETAIN IS RELATED TO USER-PROVIDED FEEDBACK (BUG REPORTS, SUGGESTIONS, EMAIL COMMUNICATIONS). HOWEVER, THE APP DOES USE THIRD-PARTY SERVICES, CODE PORTIONS AND LIBRARIES THAT MAY COLLECT INFORMATION THAT COULD POTENTIALLY BE USED TO IDENTIFY YOU.

The API keys provided by you in the Settings panel of the app are retained on your device and are used only to query your account balance on third-party Exchanges such as Bittrex, Kraken, Bitstamp, Poloniex, Bitfinex, HitBTC, Binance, Coinbase, GDAX.

The Wallet Addresses provided by you are retained on your device and are used only to query your wallet balance through public third-party services such as Blockchain.info and Etherscan.io.

You can view the Privacy Policy of third-party services in the next section. You are also informed that in case of unexpected errors occurring during the use of the App, we collect data and information related to your device also through third party products. This data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the App settings, time and date of use of the App, and other statistics.
